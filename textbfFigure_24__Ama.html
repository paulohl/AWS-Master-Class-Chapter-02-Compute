Real-World Case Study: Expedia’s ECS Transformation

Expedia , a global travel platform, successfully migrated their containerized applications to Amazon ECS. Their platform sees massive spikes in traffic during vacation seasons. By leveraging ECS’s scaling capabilities and AWS Fargate for serverless container deployment, they reduced their infrastructure costs by 40%. ECS enabled them to maintain high availability during peak demand [13], ensuring seamless user experiences.
