Combining EC2 Spot Instances with On-Demand and Reserved Instances can offer a hybrid solution, ensuring critical workloads maintain uptime while non-critical tasks benefit from cost savings.

Example: Genome research on EC2 Spot Instances

In the field of genomics, Spot Instances have been instrumental in reducing the computational costs associated with DNA sequencing and analysis. A major research institution reduced its compute costs by 60% while processing terabytes of genomic data by strategically utilizing EC2 Spot Instances for non-time-sensitive tasks, such as genome assembly and variant analysis. By integrating Spot Instances with AWS Batch, the institution efficiently queued jobs and managed workloads, maximizing throughput while adhering to budget constraints.
