Local Zones: Expanding cloud resources locally

In cloud computing, latency and proximity to end-users play crucial roles in delivering optimal performance. AWS Local Zones serve as a strategic solution for organizations needing cloud resources closer to their on-premises data centers or users, offering low-latency access to a subset of AWS services.
